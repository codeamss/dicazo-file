Course Outline

Design, computer graphics, and art fundamentals are all covered, as are drawing fundamentals, still life, colour theory, etc. Rotoscoping, colour correction, expressions, keying, tracking, and stabilising are all part of visual effects compositing. The course material also includes a study of camera motion, firing directly into the camera, Rotoscopy, and wire cutting.

Course Outcome

After finishing it, the students will have a better understanding of how to analyse various colours, forms, lights, and camera effects when processing images. They will easily understand the practical application. The complete process of creating the portfolio will be taught to the students.
